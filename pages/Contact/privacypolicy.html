Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/2023

Lock Champions Locksmith (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services or visit our website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, billing address, and payment details when you book our services.
  • Service Information: Details about your service requests, location, and communication preferences.
  • Website Data: Non-personal information like IP address, browser type, and usage patterns collected via cookies.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and manage locksmith services.
  • Communicate with you about your requests or inquiries.
  • Improve our website, services, and customer support.
  • Ensure secure transactions and prevent fraud.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties that assist in providing our services (e.g., payment processors).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • You may opt-out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.
  • You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on our website with the updated effective date.
