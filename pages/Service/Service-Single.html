Frequently Asked Questions

How long does lock installation take?

The duration of lock installation depends on factors such as the type of lock and the complexity of the installation. Our locksmiths strive to complete installations efficiently while maintaining quality.

Can you install locks on all types of doors?

Yes, we provide lock installation services for a wide range of doors, including wooden, metal, uPVC, and composite doors.

Do you offer warranty for lock installations?

We stand behind the quality of our workmanship and offer warranties for lock installations. Please contact us for more details about our warranty terms.

What types of locks do you install?

We install a variety of locks, including deadbolts, cylinder locks, mortise locks, smart locks, and more. Our locksmiths can recommend the most suitable locks based on your specific needs.
